Presentation of BTS SIO
SISR (Infrastructure Solutions, Systems, and Networks)

This option focuses on the technical aspects of computing, emphasizing the design, implementation, and management of IT infrastructures. Students choosing this specialty learn to administer operating systems, configure networks, manage IT services (such as servers, databases, etc.), ensure systems and networks security, and solve technical problems related to these areas.

Learn more
SLAM (Software Solutions and Business Applications)

This option focuses on software development and the creation of business applications to meet the specific needs of organizations. Students choosing this specialty learn to design, develop, test, and deploy computer applications, using various programming languages and frameworks. They also gain skills in business needs analysis, IT project management, and evolutionary maintenance of applications.

Tools and Software
Docker

Docker is a lightweight virtualization platform that enables developers to create, deploy, and run applications efficiently and consistently, regardless of the environment. Through its containers, Docker isolates applications from their underlying environment, ensuring maximum portability and simplified management. Docker containers include everything needed for the application to run, so it can be executed reliably on any computer. With Docker, developers can develop, test, and deploy applications quickly and seamlessly, avoiding issues related to environmental differences between development and production machines.

Learn more
GLPI

GLPI is an open-source IT service management and asset management platform. It provides a comprehensive solution for tracking hardware and software resources, managing support tickets and user requests, as well as managing contracts and suppliers. GLPI enables organizations to better organize their IT infrastructures, optimize support and maintenance processes, and improve the overall productivity of the IT team. With its user-friendly interface and modular features, GLPI is a flexible and scalable solution for businesses of all sizes.
